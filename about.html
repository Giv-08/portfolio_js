About Me

"Cooking Up Code: An 8-Year Chef's Transition to Software Engineering. Merging creativity and discipline from the culinary world, I'm diving into software engineering to develop solutions that make a real difference."

I am a passionate career switcher who has transitioned from an 8-year journey as a chef to the dynamic world of technology. My career in hospitality was deeply fulfilling, but my curiosity about technology particularly the wholesale applications I used daily sparked a new interest in how digital tools can transform our lives.

This fascination led me to dive into programming, teaching myself languages like JavaScript and Python. The decision to leave my comfort zone behind and embrace a new career path was daunting, but it was driven by a desire to understand and build technologies that make a real difference. In exploring the tech field, I discovered a profound love for frontend development and web design. There’s something incredibly satisfying about creating and shaping user experiences from the ground up, and I thrive on the challenge of bringing ideas to life through code.

My background as a chef has equipped me with a wealth of soft skills that I’m excited to apply in my new career. Skills such as problem-solving, attention to detail, and effective communication have been invaluable and will continue to serve me well in tech. I’m also a firm believer in the power of persistence and continuous learning, which drives me to constantly improve and adapt in this ever-evolving field. As I embark on this new chapter, I’m eager to leverage my unique blend of experiences to contribute to innovative projects and make a meaningful impact in the tech industry.
